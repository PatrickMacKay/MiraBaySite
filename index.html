About Us

Mira Bay Engineering Ltd. is a locally owned engineering practice providing practical, efficient, and client-focused structural engineering services. Whether you're planning a renovation, addition, deck, or commercial fit-up, we deliver solutions that are safe, buildable, and aligned with local code requirements.
