About Us

Mira Bay Engineering Ltd. is a locally owned structural engineering practice working all throughout Nova Scotia and dedicated to providing practical, efficient and client-focused structural engineering services for residential and small commercial projects. We focus on clear communication, practical designs, and fast turnaround times so you can move forward with confidence — whether you’re planning a renovation, addition, deck, garage, or commercial fit‑up.

Our Services

Residential Services

• New home construction
• Review and evaluation of existing homes
• Concrete slab-on-grade designs for houses, garages and secondary suites
• Foundation design, repairs and assessments
• Tall wall design
• Load-bearing wall evaluation and removal
• Timber, steel and LVL beam designs
• Permit-ready structural drawing packages including relevant permitting documents

Commercial Services

• Structural design for new small commercial buildings
• Structural evaluation and fit-up of existing buildings
• Structural permitting forms and documents as required by local building authorities
• Renovations, upgrades and structural modifications to existing buildings
